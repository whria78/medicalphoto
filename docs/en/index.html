What is MedicalPhoto ?

 MedicalPhoto is a non-commercial medical image management program.


 MedicalPhoto was originally developed for dermatologists but can also be used by plastic surgeons and dentists. It was first created for dermatology use at Asan Medical Center in 2007. With 200–300 clinical photos being captured daily, the program was designed as an efficient and user-friendly photo management tool. Organizing clinical photos often involves repetitive tasks. As the volume of clinical photographs grows over time, finding a specific image in Windows can become time-consuming. However, with MedicalPhoto’s built-in database, photos can be retrieved in less than a second. MedicalPhoto includes ICD-10 diagnoses as well as most diagnoses found in dermatology textbooks. Users can also define custom diagnoses or procedure names if they are not already available as predefined terms and tag photos accordingly. The software supports multiple monitors, allowing 2–3 screens to be used for side-by-side photo comparisons. Additionally, MedicalPhoto operates on a client-server structure, enabling access from multiple computers within an office.
